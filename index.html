ABOUT US

We are JG.PH, a young couple, and aspiring photographers. We are full-time college students from Stockton, CA. As the creative individuals that we are, we appreciate all kinds of art, especially photography. Asides from our personal photography, we enjoy photographing others for special occasions because we value the importance of self-expression and capturing memories that will be cherished for a lifetime. We love ensuring that everyone has a positive and comfortable experience when taking photos. Thank you to everyone that supports us and allows us to pursue our passion.
-Juan Guillen and Priscilla Huerta
